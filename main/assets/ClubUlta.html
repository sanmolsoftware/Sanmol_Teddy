The Club at ULTA rewards program is our way of thanking our loyal customers for choosing ULTA. As a member, you'll be rewarded with exclusive offers sent to you via email or catalogs. The program is free; simply shop like you always do - and get rewarded for it. Pretty amazing.


The ABCs of earning gifts


Shop for what you love, and earn free gifts. You'll get a fabulous rewards certificate from ULTA every quarter based on your spending. You'll earn a certificate level for every $50 spent on beauty. The more you spend, the higher your certificate level will be. Once ULTA serves up your certificate, you have six weeks to redeem it in-store for beauty products from our menu of rewards items. Pretty fabulous.


The 123s of redeeming gifts


Receive free beauty products or salon services. Bring in your Reward Certificate (found on the front cover of your ULTA catalog) and choose one item from the corresponding levels listed in the rewards insert from your ULTA catalog. Members with Level 7 and above are able to redeem in any combination equal to their Reward Certificate Level.


Earning periods:


Spend during October 1 to December 31, and we'll send your certificate in February

Spend during January 1 to February 28, and we'll send your certificate in April

Spend during March 1 to June 30, and we'll send your certificate in August

Spend during July 1 to September 30, and we'll send your certificate in November


Certificate level calculation:


At the end of each earning period, rewards certificates are issued based on how much you've spent.

Levels increase in $50 increments.

Level 1: Spend $50 per earning period

Level 2: Spend $100 per earning period

Level 3: Spend $150 per earning period

Level 4: Spend $200 per earning period

Level 5: Spend $250 per earning period

Level 6: Spend $300 per earning period

And so on...
